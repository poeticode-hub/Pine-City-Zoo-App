ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Gorillas (Gorilla spp.) are the largest primates and are native to the forests of central Africa. They are known for their strong build, with broad chests and long arms. Gorillas are primarily herbivores, feeding on leaves, stems, fruit, and flowers. They live in groups called troops, led by a dominant male known as a silverback.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the gorilla at pen G18, near the forest habitat zone.

WHAT DO THEY EAT?

Gorillas are herbivores and primarily feed on leaves, stems, fruit, and flowers. They are known to forage on a variety of vegetation, including shrubs and trees in their forest habitat.

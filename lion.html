ANIMALS MAP PLACES

LION

Lions (Panthera leo) are large carnivorous mammals that are native to Africa and parts of Asia. They are known for their powerful build, with males possessing a distinctive mane. Lions are social animals, living in groups called prides, which are typically made up of related females and their offspring, along with a small number of adult males.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the lion at pen G15, near the savannah habitat zone.

WHAT DO THEY EAT?

Lions are carnivores and primarily feed on large ungulates, such as zebras and wildebeests. They are known to hunt in groups, using teamwork to take down their prey.

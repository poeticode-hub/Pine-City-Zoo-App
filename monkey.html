ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY

Monkeys are highly intelligent primates known for their playful behavior and social structures. They are often found in tropical rainforests, savannas, and mountainous regions. Monkeys are known for their agility and ability to swing through trees using their long limbs and prehensile tails.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the monkeys at pen M03, near the rainforest habitat zone.

WHAT DO THEY EAT?

Monkeys are herbivores and primarily feed on grasses, leaves, and shrubs. They are known to forage on a variety of vegetation, including fruits and nuts.
